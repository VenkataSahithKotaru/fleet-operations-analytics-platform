## 📖 Project Overview

Fleet Operations & Travel Analytics Platform is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution developed using MySQL and Power BI. The project transforms operational travel data into meaningful business insights through SQL analytics and interactive dashboards.

The platform enables management to monitor fleet performance, customer behavior, route profitability, maintenance activities, driver performance, and financial health, supporting data-driven business decisions.

## 🎯 Problem Statement

ABC Travels is a growing fleet and travel service provider operating buses, cars, and tempos across multiple tourism routes, including beach, devotional, airport, corporate, and vacation services. Over the years, the company has accumulated thousands of trip records involving customers, drivers, vehicles, maintenance activities, fuel consumption, operational expenses, and financial transactions.

Despite having extensive operational data, the management faced challenges in converting this information into meaningful business insights. Decision-making relied heavily on manual reports and spreadsheets, making it difficult to answer critical business questions such as:

- Which vehicles generate the highest revenue and profit?
- Which routes and tourism categories perform the best?
- Are maintenance and fuel expenses impacting profitability?
- Which drivers contribute most to business growth?
- Which customers generate the highest revenue and repeat business?
- What factors are causing the company to operate at a loss?

The absence of a centralized Business Intelligence solution limited the organization's ability to monitor performance, optimize operations, reduce costs, and make timely strategic decisions.

To address these challenges, a comprehensive Fleet Operations & Travel Analytics Platform was developed using MySQL and Power BI, enabling management to analyze operational data through interactive dashboards and make data-driven business decisions.
